Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Find answers to the most common questions about our internet services, installation process, pricing, coverage, and more. Still need help? Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

What internet packages do you offer?

We offer a variety of affordable packages tailored for homes, offices, and businesses — including fiber, wireless, and custom solutions. You can choose based on your speed and usage needs.

How long does installation take?

Once confirmed, installation is typically completed within 3–5 hours. Our technician visits your location and sets up the complete system professionally.

Is there any data limit or speed throttling?

No. All of our packages come with unlimited data and no speed throttling. You get what you pay for — full speed all the time.

What support is available if I face issues?

We provide 24/7 customer support via phone, WhatsApp. Most problems are resolved quickly via remote access or technician visit if needed.

Do you provide services in rural or remote areas?

Yes, we provide wireless internet to areas where fiber isn’t available. Contact us with your location, and we’ll confirm availability.

Can I upgrade or change my package later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or customize your package anytime — just call or message our support.
